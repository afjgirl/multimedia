Blackwattle Bay

Blackwattle Bay is a public space that overlooks the bay and has a view of the Anzac Bridge. It is connected to Rozelle Bay, Jubilee Park, the Fish Markets, Glebe and White Bay. There are eateries, residency and parks that lets people enjoy the bay.
